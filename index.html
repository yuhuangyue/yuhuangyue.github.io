Huangyue Yu

I am a research engineer at the BIGAI, General Vision Lab, focusing on 3D scene understanding, 3D scene generation, and embodied intelligence. My work aims to build systems capable of perceiving, reconstructing, and generating realistic environments to support intelligent agents. I received my Master (2021) degrees from Beihang University.
